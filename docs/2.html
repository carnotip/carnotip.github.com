Carnot is not slideshare

In response to Micah Baldwin's query

Isn't is slideshare?

Unlike slide-share 
It is independent of adobe flash player .

"unblocked flash , good net speed and low cost at the same time", is a pain for students here],

It allows you to tag slides with various concepts and interlink them semantically

just like you tag your photos on face-book and relate those tags.
Hence more engaging.especially for newbies to subjects.

Here user navigates a web of related academic concepts

zoom-in/out of slides dedicated to that concept is a click away
